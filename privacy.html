Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2024

1. Data Collection

The Receipt Generator is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data entered into the application to external servers. All information provided (School Name, Address, Receipt Details, and Logos) is stored locally on your device in a configuration file named receipt_config.json.

2. Local Storage

Your settings and export history remain strictly on your computer. If you delete the application and its configuration file, all stored data will be permanently removed. We do not have access to your saved receipts or school information.

3. Website Usage

Our website (suahco4.online) may use standard analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to monitor site traffic and improve user experience. This data is anonymous and does not include information entered into the software itself.

4. Third-Party Links

The application includes links to our website and update servers. These connections are used solely to check for the latest software version and provide support resources.

5. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact us at mr.suah19@gmail.com.
